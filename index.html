Diversity of Accounting Research

Hilton Toronto Hotel

Toronto, Ontario

Thursday, May 28–Sunday, May 31, 2015

We invite you to join us in Toronto for the 2015 CAAA Annual Conference. Accounting research encompasses a variety of accounting contexts (such as capital markets, accounting history, managerial and financial accounting, auditing, standard setting, not-for-profit and government accounting to name a few areas) and employs a variety of methodological approaches (i.e. quantitative, qualitative, critical studies, etc.). The goal of this year’s conference is to showcase the pluralism of accounting research and, as such, we invite submissions of papers which, together, will reflect the plurality of accounting research. We also encourage the submission of studies in accounting education and teaching cases.

The Canadian Academic Accounting Association is an organization of accounting educators, professional accountants, and others who are involved in, or concerned about, research and education in accounting and related areas.

We look forward to seeing you in Toronto!

Sincerely,
Ron Baker
Chair, CAAA Annual Conference 2015
